Game Rules

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he whishes. Each result gets added to his ROUND SCORES
  • If a player rolls a 1, all his ROUND SCORES are lost and then It's the Next player's turn
  • Important! The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND SCORES will be added to his TOTAL SCORES. After that, It's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach 100 points on TOTAL SCORE wins the game
